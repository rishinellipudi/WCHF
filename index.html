War Crime Historical Foundation

The WCHF is an organization dedicated to documenting, preserving, and educating the public about historical war crimes

Introduction

War Crimes

War Crimes are actions carried out during times of war that violate the rules of war by being inhumane.

Examples

Historical examples: Unit 731, Holocaust, Rape of Nanjing, Agent Orange, Hiroshima, Nagasaki, Einsatzgruppen, Armenian Genocide, Rwandan Genocide, Belgian Congo, Cambodian Genocide, Dresden, and many more.
